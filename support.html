Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new pet?

Tap the + button on the Home screen or go to More → Add Pet. Fill in your pet's name, species, breed, and birthday to get started.

How do reminders work?

Go to the Reminders tab and tap + to create a new reminder. Set the date, time, and frequency. You'll get a notification when it's time.

What's included in PawLog Premium?

Premium unlocks unlimited pet profiles and advanced features. Available as a monthly ($2.99/mo) or yearly ($19.99/yr) subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → PawLog → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep premium access until the end of your billing period.

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device. We recommend keeping regular iPhone backups through iCloud or your computer.
