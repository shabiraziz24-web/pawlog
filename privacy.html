🐾 Privacy Policy

PawLog — Pet Health Tracker

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

PawLog ("the App") is developed by Shabir Aziz. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it's used, and your rights.

Data Collection

PawLog stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

The data you enter in the App includes:

  • Pet profiles (names, breeds, birthdays, photos)
  • Health records (weight, vaccinations, vet visits)
  • Reminders and medication schedules
  • Emergency card information

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Your pet's information stays on your device.

Analytics & Tracking

PawLog does not use any analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Subscriptions

PawLog offers optional premium subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Photos

If you choose to add photos to your pet profiles, they are stored locally on your device. The App requests photo library access only when you choose to add a photo, and images are never uploaded or shared.

Notifications

PawLog may request permission to send local notifications for reminders. These notifications are generated on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Children's Privacy

PawLog does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App stores data locally and does not require account creation.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at thepawlog@gmail.com.
